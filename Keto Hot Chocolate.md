# Keto Hot Chocolate
**Yield:** 1-2 drinks
**Prep Time:** 5 min
**Cook Time:** 15 min

## Ingredients
### Hot Chocolate
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 tbsp cocoa powder
- 2.5-3 tbsp Lily’s dark chocolate chips
- 2.5-3 tbsp Lily’s semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 1 tsp granulated sweetener (monk fruit or stevia in the raw)

### Whipped Cream
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 tsp granulated sweetener

## Procedure
1. Combined ingredients for hot chocolate
2. Heat on stove over medium heat until simmering and all chocolate chips are melted
3. While heating combine cream and sweetener for whipped cream in bowl. Whisk until firm.
4. Pour drink into glass and top with whipped cream.
