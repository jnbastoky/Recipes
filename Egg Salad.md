# Egg Salad
**Yield:** 4 servings
**Prep Time:** 15 min
**Cook Time:** 15 min

## Ingredients
- 6 eggs (315 g)
- 70 g mayonnaise
- 10 g mustard
- fresh dill
- smoked paprika
- salt
- pepper

## Procedure
1. Boil eggs 10 min (?)
2. Cool eggs in ice bath
3. Peel and press eggs with large serving fork
4. Mix mayonnaise, mustard and spices
