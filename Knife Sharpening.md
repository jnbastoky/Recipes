# Knife Sharpening
This is a summary of sharpening steps for a traditional knife on the ChefChoice Model 15XV electric sharpener.

- Start on left side on odd years
- Start on right side on even years

## Sharpening (stage 1)
1. Pull blade through left and right slots (3-4 sec/pull for 5” blade)
2. Check for burr on last side of pull along entire length
3. Repeat until burr is present

## Honing (stage 2)
1. 1 or 2 pair of pulls (3 sec/pull for 5” blade)
2. Check for burr
3. Repeat until burr is present

## Stropping (stage 3)
1. 3 pairs of pulls (3-4 sec/pull for 5” blade)
2. 2 pairs of pulls (1 sec/pull for 5” blade)
3. Check sharpness

## Notes
- For meat you can skip stage 2
