# Cole Slaw
**Yield:** 4 servings
**Prep Time:** 5 min
**Cook Time:** 5 min

## Ingredients
- 5 oz slaw mix
- 1.5 oz mayonnaise
- 1.5 tbsp pickle juice
- 1 tbsp gochujang
- garlic powder
- onion powder
- salt
- pepper
- celery seed

## Procedure
1. Mix mayonnaise, pickle juice, gochujang, onion, and seasonings
2. Mix in slaw mix
